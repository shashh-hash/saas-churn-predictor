Disengagement kills silently.

Last Login Days is the #1 predictor. Clients who haven't logged in for 60+ days are 3× more likely to churn — reach out before they go fully dark.

NPS is a leading indicator, not a lagging one.

An NPS below 5, combined with rising support tickets, is a reliable early signal — not a post-mortem. Treat it as an alert, not a retrospective.

Clients with complete onboarding and a dedicated CSM churn at nearly half the rate. The ROI on early investment in success pays compounding dividends.
