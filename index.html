Feature Importance Insights

Which signals matter most in predicting B2B SaaS churn

Feature Importance (Random Forest)

🔴
Disengagement is the #1 signal.

Last Login Days is the strongest predictor. Clients who haven't logged in for 60+ days are 3× more likely to churn — reach out before they go dark.

🟠
NPS + support tickets reveal sentiment.

Low NPS scores and a spike in support tickets together signal frustration. A client scoring NPS < 5 with >10 tickets is in critical risk territory.

🟢
Onboarding and CSM assignment are protective.

Clients with completed onboarding and a dedicated CSM churn at nearly half the rate. Investing in early success pays off long-term.
